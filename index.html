Noise Trees is repetitive long form sound for immersive use.

You can get in touch for collaboration and things.

Drone Study

(Post-Rift, 2023) is 31 drone pieces that began as a series of audiovisual works for a creativity challenge, but soon turned into further explorations into the theme. The end result is unnecessarily long for an album, but just right for a study in long-form sound.

Live at Maja Ambient Three

(Post-Rift, 2022) is a recording of a live performance at Maja Ambient Three, an experimental electronic music festival, held in September 2022 in Helsinki. The performance includes versions of released Noise Trees songs as well as previously unreleased material.

Ghost Piano, part 2

(Post-Rift, 2020) is the second part of the Ghost Piano series. Five Noise Trees songs and a remix of each by another five beautifully inspiring artists: Per Barfot, Graeme Wylie, mafmadmaf, Hélène Vogelsinger, and Pollypraha.

Paper Songs

(Wow and Flutter, 2020) is an EP of five songs, building upon a piano, a few synthesizers and some field recordings.

Ghost Piano, part 1

(Post-Rift, 2018) is the debut Noise Trees album, starting the collaborative Ghost Piano series. Five songs and a remix of each by five wonderfully inspiring artists: Satoi, Karkaa, n-So, r beny, and Elin Piel.
