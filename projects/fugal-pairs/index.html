Noise Trees — Fugal Pairs (work in progress)

It's an album of five collaborative pieces, pairing Noise Trees with Ed Blomquist, Helen G, Secret States, Duelling Ants, and Renegog. It explores the pairing of long-form droning tones with improvisational instrumentation by collaborators.

Pieces

1. Sleep (with Ed Blomquist)

Noise Trees: Inspired by the slow movement and sustained tones of La Monte Young, Sunn O))), John Cale, and of course Sleep. An exploration in richness of both harmony and distorted simple waveforms. Neutral Labs Elmyra 2 and Make Noise Strega were used as sound sources. Meris Ottobit Jr, Chase Bliss Generation Loss mk2, Chase Bliss Mood mk2, Electroharmonix Stereo Memory Man, and Elektron Analog Heat were used as sound processors. Recorded and built in Ableton Live.
